Kaushik Shankar – Home

 

Welcome! This is the homepage of Kaushik Shankar. I was born and brought up in Chennai, a coastal city in Tamil Nadu, the southernmost state of India. I completed my secondary education at DAV Senior Secondary School, Chennai. I attended the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay for pursuing my undergraduate study in Chemical Engineering.

I am currently a 4th-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania. I am co-advised by Dr. Scott Diamond and Dr. Talid Sinno. I employ computational tools to predict patient-specific clot formation for my research.
